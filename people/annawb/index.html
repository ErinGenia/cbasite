Anna Waldman-Brown

Anna is a Masters student in MIT's Technology Policy Program, and a research assistant at the Legatum Center for Development and Entrepreneurship - where she's investigating the viability of local manufacturing and inclusive strategies for industrialization. She has worked with Autodesk, the Fab Lab network, international policy-makers, and grassroots innovators across 50+ countries to foster creative problem-solving and sustainable development. She is a Fulbright fellow and holds Bachelors of Science in Physics and Writing And Humanistic Studies from MIT.
