What am I going to do and why?

My laptop is large and causes back problems when looking at it, so I decided to make a parametrically configured laptop stand. By configuring the LaptopWidth, LaptopHeight, and Thickness, I could print laptop stands for anyone who wanted one.

Furthermore, I wanted to make this modular so we could switch out the top piece for more functionality (Desktop, Fan, Heatsink, etc)

I designed the laptop side pieces, support, and top structure in Fusion 360

In my first iteration I made the notches too large for the cardboard(0.25in) and then reduced them to 0.15in (or 3.9mm)
